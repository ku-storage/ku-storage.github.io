🎬 Videos

  • ✅Repost on any platform with credit (@ku_storage), non-monetised accounts with <1000 followers
  • 🚫Reposting after editing
  • 🚫Reposting on platforms I have accounts on, or with monetised accounts (without permission)
  • 🚫Commercial use or claiming as your own
  • 🚫Use in AI training datasets

🎨 Illustrations & Screenshots

  • ✅Repost with credit (@ku_storage), non-monetised <1000 followers
  • ✅Profile pic / banner / wallpaper on personal accounts <1000 followers (credit appreciated)
  • ✅Printing for personal use
  • ✅Studying / referencing with credit when posting publicly
  • 🚫Reposting after editing
  • 🚫Printing for sale or mass gifting
  • 🚫Commercial use or competitions
  • 🚫Use in AI training datasets

other notes / 其他

  • I'm a hobbyist; I'm not looking to do any of this professionally and I'm fine with where I am (please no unsolicited criticism).
  • I love Vocaloid and started a Vocaloid club at my university!
  • Since I do all of this for fun, I only want to work/interact with people I wouldn't feel uncomfortable around.
